Detailed Description

Defines the allowed maximum position an account can have on the base asset of a symbol. BUY orders will be rejected if the account's position is greater than the maximum position allowed. An account's position defined as the sum of the account's:

  1. free balance of the base asset
  2. locked balance of the base asset
  3. sum of the qty of all open BUY orders
